Welcome to SchoolShare!

SchoolShare is a website alternative for online lecture calls, completely contained within your browser. Perfect for those with limited hardware, or are looking for an easier way to host video calls, lectures, or even study groups. The project was designed and made during the 2020 HooHacks Hackathon, sponsored by MLH.

Goals

Our goal for this project by the end of the Hackathon and continuing with it beyond it, is to develop an easier, less clunky way of hosting video calls or video lectures in comparison to the current systems and applications we have. A reincarnation of Rabb.it, for educational purposes. Also we wanted to try and create this alternative as a learning experience for our group to expand our skillset into web development

Implementation

Our project was implemented using MySQL, Node.js, Javascript, and various online APIs for our backend for databasing, webcam and video functionality . Where we utilized Bootstrap with HTML+CSS sprinkled in with some Javascript for the entirety of the site development and frontend.

Background

We wanted to create a viable alternative to the current call programs like Zoom, Skype, Microsoft Teams, and Discord that was entirely web-based for ease of use. Considering the current ongoing Pandemic, and the closure of most public schools and universities in the nation, we wanted to try and implement a program to help alleviate the issue with the transition to online lectures, giving teachers and professors more tools and functionalities to hold calls and such.
